# Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients:

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 4 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- pinch of salt

## Instructions:

1. Beat butter, sugar and brown sugar together.
2. add eggs.
3. sift in flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
4. Mix until combined.
5. Bake at 375 for 7 min.
