# Steve's Best Chili

## Ingredients:

- 1 lb. extra lean ground beef (or ground turkey)
- 1 large kielbasa sausage
- 3 (15 oz) cans chili beans (don't drain)
- 1 (ls oz) cons black beans (don't drain)

## Instructions:
