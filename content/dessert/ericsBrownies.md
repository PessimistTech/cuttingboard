# Erics Brownies

## Ingredients:

- 2 C sugar
- 1 C Butter
- 4 Eggs
- 1/4 C cocoa powder
- 1 1/4 C Flour
- 2 tsp Vanilla
- 1/2 tsp Salt

## Instructions:

1. Cream sugar, butter, and eggs. 
2. Add the rest of ingredients, and pour into 9x13 pan. 
3. Bake 350 for 30 min.
