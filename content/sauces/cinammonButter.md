# Cinnamon Butter

## Ingredients:

- 1/2 c Butter
- 1 c Powdered Sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp Cinammon
- 2 tsp Milk
 
## Instructions:

1. Cream together butter and sugar
2. Add cinammon and milk 
3. Mix until combined
