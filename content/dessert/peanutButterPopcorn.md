# Peanut Butter Popcorn

## Ingredients:

- 1/2 C Corn Syrup
- 1/2 C Sugar
- 1/2 C Peanut Butter
- 1 t Vanilla
- 3 quarts popped popcorn

## Instructions:

1. Bring corn syrup and sugar to boil 
2. Remove from heat and add peanut butter and vanilla. 
3. Stir until creamy. 
4. Pour over popcorn and stir to coat.
