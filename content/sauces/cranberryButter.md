# Cranberry Butter

## Ingredients:

- 1/2 c Cranberries
- 1 3/4 c Powdered Sugar 
- 1/2 c Buter
- 1 TB orange juice

## Instructions:

1. Blend cranberries and sugar
2. Add Butter and orange juice
3. Blend until smooth
